Breaking News :UGC conferred Autonomous Status to SMVITM, Bantakal

Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM), Bantakal, a Unit of SODE Group of Institutions, Udupi has reached a significant milestone by attaining Autonomous Status under VTU and receiving NBA Accreditation to all eligible programs. The University Grants Commission (UGC), New Delhi, after evaluating the academic quality of the institute and upon the recommendation of the Higher Education Council, Government of Karnataka and Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, has granted SMVITM autonomous status for 10 years, effective from the academic year 2025–26. Recently, all our eligible undergraduate BE programs – Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering — have been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), the apex body responsible for assessing the quality of UG and PG engineering programs. In addition, the institute has been accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) too, with A Grade! We are recognised by NIRF Innovation Ranking in the band of 151-300 under Innovation and Entrepreneurship.

Orientation Program for First-Year B.E. Students Held at SMVITM – 05 October 2025

Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM), Bantakal, organized an Orientation Program for the newly admitted B.E. students on Sunday, 05 October 2025, at the institute premises. The program was inaugurated by lighting the lamp by H. H. Shri Vishwavallaba Teertha Swamiji of Shri Sode Vadiraja Mutt, in the presence of the Chief Guest, Sri Prabhav N. Rao, Managing Director of Leapfrog Services, Bengaluru. Sri Prabhav N. Rao shared insights into his entrepreneurial journey, motivating the students to dream big and pursue their goals with determination to become successful individuals and future entrepreneurs. In his presidential address, H. H. Shri Vishwavallaba Teertha Swamiji inspired the students to focus on their interests, excel in their chosen fields, and bring pride to their parents, institution, and nation.
